Pike's Peak Hike

Group hike this weekend

Free group hike for anyone who is interested in summitting a Pike's Peak. We will meet at the trailhead and set out as a group. Parking is limited, so get there early! Hike at your own pace; this is a 23 mile round-trip hike. Bring plenty of food and water, and dress warm! This is a very challenging hike, so make sure you are well-rested and don't be afraid to stop and head down if you have to.

When: 10/15/23 @ 5:30 AM

Where: Barr Trailhead, Manitou Springs, CO

To learn more about this hike, visit AllTrails.com
